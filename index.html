Saylani Blood Bank

July 18, 2017


Saylani Blood Bank Is a charitable medical institution that provides safe blood to the community at large with the support of volunteering blood donors.

Saylani blood bank provides blood transfusion services and iron chelation therapy to Thalassemia patients free of cost.

Saylani blood bank also provides health maintenances services to patients with bleeding and clotting disorders free of cost with association Haemophilia society.
